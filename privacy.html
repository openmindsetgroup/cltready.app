Privacy Policy

Effective April 10, 2026 · Last Updated April 10, 2026

Open Mindset Group LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the CLT Ready mobile application and website at cltready.app (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

CLT Ready is an independent test-preparation app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Classic Learning Initiatives, LLC or the Classical Learning Test (CLT).

1.Who This Service Is For

CLT Ready is intended for students preparing for the Classical Learning Test (the main CLT exam, typically taken by students in grades 11–12). The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13.

At account creation, users must affirm they are 13 years of age or older by checking a box. Any account we discover to belong to a user under 13 will be terminated and associated data deleted. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us personal information, contact us at cltready@openmindsetgroup.com and we will delete it promptly.

2.Information We Collect

We collect only what the Service needs to function. We do not collect date of birth, age beyond the 13+ attestation, government IDs, payment card details, precise geolocation, analytics telemetry, crash reports, advertising identifiers, or biometric data.

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: email address and display name. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your email and name from that provider.
  • Onboarding responses: your target CLT test date range, the section you consider weakest, your score goal, and whether you have taken the CLT before. These are used to personalize your practice.

2.2 Information Generated Through Use

  • Practice performance: your answers to practice questions, section scores, correct/incorrect results per question, time spent per question and per session, and timestamps.

2.3 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Local app state: onboarding progress, current quiz state, app launch count, and authentication tokens. This information is stored in AsyncStorage on your device and is cleared when you uninstall the app or delete your account from within the app.

2.4 Information We Do Not Collect in This Version

CLT Ready v1.0 does not collect parent or guardian contact information, and does not provide parent progress reports. If we add these features in a future version, we will update this Privacy Policy and notify users before the features go live.

3.How We Use Your Information

We use the information above to:

  • Provide and operate the Service (deliver practice questions, score attempts, track progress).
  • Personalize your study experience based on onboarding responses and performance.
  • Maintain and secure your account, including authentication.
  • Process and manage your subscription, if you purchase one.
  • Communicate with you about the Service (account notices, policy updates, support replies).
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information with advertisers. We do not use your information to train artificial intelligence models.

5.Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security, meaning each user can only access their own records. Data is transmitted over TLS. Authentication tokens are stored in AsyncStorage on your device.

No system is perfectly secure. We use commercially reasonable safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the data, but we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6.Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as your account is active.

  • Account deletion: you can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen within the app.
  • Cascade deletion: when you delete your account, we immediately delete your authentication record from Supabase Auth along with all associated attempts, answers, and onboarding data. Local AsyncStorage on your device is also cleared.
  • No retention period: deleted data is removed immediately, not queued or archived.
  • Backups: routine database backups may retain data for a limited period before being overwritten in the ordinary course of backup rotation.
